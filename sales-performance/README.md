# Sales & Profit Margin Performance Dashboard (SALES2)

## 📌 Project Overview
A comprehensive dashboard detailing overall sales trends, product rankings, and profitability analysis to support regional sales managers in strategic planning.

## 🎯 Key Questions Answered
*   Who are the top 10 customers by revenue and volume?
*   Which regions are underperforming in profit margin despite high sales volume?
*   How do current sales compare against the quarterly targets?

## 🛠️ Data & Tech Stack
*   **Data Source**: Multi-regional sales invoice logs.
*   **Transformations**: Resolved currency conversions and unified regional sales tables.
*   **DAX Formulas**: Developed measures for Profit Margin %, Dynamic Target Variance, and Cumulative Sales.
